DESCRIPTION

The Y Home is an elegant and practical home interior designed for a family of three. On a compact 105 sqm design area, the space maximizes functionality with a modern and cozy aesthetic. Features include a dry garden element, clean bedroom and bathroom designs, and decorative lighting throughout, creating a warm atmosphere for family and friends.
