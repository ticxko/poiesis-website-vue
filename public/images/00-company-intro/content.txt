INTRODUCTORY NOTE

Poiesis Studio is a Jakarta-based Architecture & Interior Design consultancy founded by Mayang Ratih as Principal and Jessica Sarana as Partner. Our design methodology is focused on the process of narrative approach. We pose that before any spatial program or aesthetic form is designed, a foundational story must by established. This design approach is applied through a phenomenological understanding of the user's experience and background. So, the result is an architecture & interior design that has profound connection to its context, function and most importantly to the people it serves.

Poiesis Studio is dedicated to the thoughtful act of making, facilitating a good conversation and exploration in every aspect of design.

Quote: "At Poiesis Studio, we believe that spaces should have a soul. They aren't just built from materials, but from personal stories and dreams of the people who live in them."
— Ar. Amita Ratih Purnamasari, S.Ars, IAI. (Mayang Ratih), Principal

ABOUT COMPANY
PT. Pencipta Organik Imaji

Company Overview:
Poiesis Studio design methodology is focused on a narrative-driven approach, where the story of our clients and their context becomes the soul of the project.

Key Achievements:
We are known for our warm and modern aesthetic, often blending clean lines with natural materials like wood and rattan to create homes that feel both stylish and soulful. We love unique spatial challenges, like designing a two-story house to feel as open and connected as a single-story home. But our work is about more than looks, we are passionate about building sustainably, bringing communities together, and being resourceful with materials. For us, it is important to listen carefully and work together to create designs that make a real, positive impact on our lives.

---

VISION

Our vision at Poiesis Studio is to become a leading architectural & interior design practice by pioneering the art of narrative-driven placemaking. We aim to create soulful, sustainable, and interconnected environments that are more than just structures, they are living ecosystems designed to elevate the human experience. This vision applies to every project we touch, from the intimate scale of a private home to the dynamic of a full urban community, ensuring each space we design has a unique identity and a lasting positive impact.

---

VALUES

1. Story-Led Design: We lead our design with story. We believe that by listening carefully, we can write a unique narrative that gives each project its soul.
2. Sustainable Connection: Our commitment to story shapes us to create sustainable environments that make a positive impact. We value to build spaces with connection. We are passionate to design open and flexible spaces that bring people together with each other and with nature.
3. Craft & Making: Our love is also grounded for the craft and making. We take a hands-on, resourceful approach of materials to our work. We find beauty in honest materials and details to ensure every space is beautifully and thoughtfully built.
