DESCRIPTION

Blending Japanese and Scandinavian design, the Grey House in Kalimalang is a simple and modern home created for a young family. Built on a compact 98 sqm site, the design uses a distinctive concrete roster facade to create a fresh, urban look while maximizing natural light and airflow.

The interior is open-plan, combining a soft palette of grey, white, and wood to create a relaxing atmosphere. With a focus on easy maintenance and functional spaces, this home is perfectly suited for a flexible city lifestyle.
