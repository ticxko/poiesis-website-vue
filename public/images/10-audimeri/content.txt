DESCRIPTION

AUDIMERI is an auditorium interior design project for IMERI FKUI (Faculty of Medicine, Universitas Indonesia). The 50 sqm space was designed for educational events and conferences, featuring warm wood elements, carefully designed lighting, and gold accents to create a refined and professional atmosphere.
